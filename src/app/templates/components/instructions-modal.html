How to Play

Goal

Guess the hidden 5-letter word in 6 tries or fewer. Each guess must be a valid word.

Game Modes

Classic Wordle

Traditional Wordle with ~2,300 common English words. Perfect for word game enthusiasts!

Disney Wordle

Disney-themed words including characters, movies, locations, and magical concepts!

Tips

  • Start with words that contain common vowels (A, E, I, O, U)
  • Use common consonants like R, S, T, L, N in your first few guesses
  • Pay attention to the keyboard color coding - it shows letter status across all guesses
  • Remember that letters can appear multiple times in a word
  • For Disney mode, think about character names, movie titles, and Disney vocabulary

Accessibility Features

  • Color-blind friendly: Different patterns help distinguish correct, present, and absent letters
  • Keyboard support: Use your physical keyboard to type and press Enter to submit
  • High contrast: Works well with system high contrast modes
  • Reduced motion: Respects your system's motion preferences

Statistics

Track your progress with detailed statistics including win percentage, current streak, and guess distribution. Compete on the global leaderboard and share your results!
